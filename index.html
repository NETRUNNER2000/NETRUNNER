Intro

Hi! I'm glad you're here. Let me introduce myself. I'm Shaahid Adams, known by the web as NETRUNNER. This is my website. Pretty cool right?

This website is a digital representation of me. I'm a programmer, mathematician, game developer and all round nerd. I love anything sci-fi and am always down for a game of Counter Strike.

I'm planning on studying Computer Science at The University of Cape Town. It's my dream to build something that will change the world. There is so much negativity right now. Why not use our programming skills to change that? Lets connect people, not just cables and change the world one line of code at time.

About

I started my programming adventure around 4 years ago, making games in the Unity Game Engine. My interest soon developed into a passion and I began my journey to learn all that I could about programming. From AI, APIs, videogames, computer vision and GUIs; down the rabbit hole I went. I've never looked back and have learnt so much more than just game development. Programming became like my superpower as I was able to solve the problems around me with code. Seeing my solutions help people and being able to teach those younger than me was enthralling.

I have much to learn, but the journey so far has been incredible. I see endless potential in technology and cannot wait to be a part of the next revolution.
